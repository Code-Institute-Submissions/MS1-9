ABOUT US

Since 1978 E. Schramm Schlosserei GmbH offers qualitative service in the field of metal work with a focus on steel construction, interior and exterior stairs, railings and balcony construction as well as steel processing. Our team consists of five experienced craftsmen as well as one motivated trainee. Head oft he company is Dipl. Ing. (FH) Stephan Schramm who is leading the company in the second generation since 1988.

Qualification
