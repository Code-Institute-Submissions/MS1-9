ABOUT US

Since 1978 E. Schramm Schlosserei GmbH offers qualitative service in the field of metal work with a focus on steel construction, interior and exterior stairs, railings and balcony construction as well as steel processing. QUALIFICATION

Since 2010 our company is registered for prequalification of construction services with register number 010.045320. Of course we are even prepared to participate in digital offer and demand services. Our craftsman are qualified for steel-processing according to DIN 18 800-7

WHO WE WORK FOR

We offer services for all kinds of individual demand with experience from the public sector as well as privately driven companies and private households. We are prepared to offer services for small scale private projects to big industrial developments.
